My Value & Superpower

I'm the kind of person people call empathetic, creative, and fast. I like keeping things simple, but never dull.

Before I write anything, I slip into the mind of whoever will read it. I look for their itch or their spark, then shape the words that speak to that place. Ideas hit me without waiting their turn, and I tend to choose the bolder door over the safe corridor. And if there is one thing I can't stand, it's endless talk with no action. Thinking is sweet. Doing is sweeter.

BHAG

My big dreams?

  • A powerful international offline IntelliJ IDEA campaign that hits developers right in the heart.
  • A fully AI-built campaign that draws strong engagement with light investment.
  • I want to run creative sessions that push teams past the usual patterns and into the brave stuff.

My Story

How I got here is a small saga. I started in the land of rules, writing technical documentation that left no place for fluff just clean language and logic. Then I drifted into travel at Booking.com and learned something delightful: people never just book a room. They worry about breakfast, dream about balconies, overthink everything. Even logistics have feelings.

Motherhood arrived with its chaotic charm. I picked up a camera, ran a blog, let life get noisy on purpose.

So when I returned to tech, I brought the whole mix with me. Precision and mess. Structure and intuition. Now I build campaigns that make sense to developers and still sound like they were written by a living, breathing human.

And yes, I have a few odd footnotes. I once translated for the executive producer of The Amazing Race. I also won a JetBrains hackathon, which I absolutely mention at least twice a year.
